About

Outfit of the Day: A delicate white babydoll mini dress, bold platform Mary Janes, burgundy nails that pop against soft pearl jewelry, and a black grunge handbag for that perfect edge. I catch my reflection and smile; I feel perfectly in tune with myself.



For me, fashion isn't just the frivolous pursuit of trends and vanity; it's a playground of self-expression. Each outfit is stitched together with creativity, authenticity, and a fierce attention to detail. These traits weave through every area of my life, including academia, where my dedication to fashion highlights transferable qualities essential for success.

Attention to Detail

is evident in my clothing choices, where I curate outfits down to the shades of my nail polish. This eye for precision translates into my work in computer science. It's often the smallest details, like a memory leak caused by forgetting to clean up loose ends, that make or break a project. The same way a mismatched bag can throw off an outfit, a small misstep in code can result in hours of debugging.



Creativity

is at the heart of my style. I find joy in mixing and matching, experimenting with styles from Y2K to Harajuku, and finding unexpected combinations that work. It's the same mindset I bring to problem-solving in tech. When tackling algorithms, I'm always searching for elegant solutions that balance efficiency with ingenuity. Fashion and code give me the freedom to build something new, whether it's an outfit or optimized solution.



Authenticity

is the thread that ties it all together. I embrace what makes me unique, letting each outfit mirror my mood and personality. This authenticity guides me as I work on projects, build community, and pursue my goals.



For me, both fashion and computer science allow me to express my perspective, connect with others, and leave an impression that’s uniquely mine. I approach every challenge with creativity, attention to detail, and an unwavering sense of self.
